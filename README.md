# Assignment-5.1

# Will the Customer Accept the Coupon?

## Summary
This project explores what factors influence whether a driver accepts a coupon delivered to their phone. The dataset comes from the UCI Machine Learning Repository and was collected via Amazon Mechanical Turk.

## Key Findings
- About 56.8% of drivers accepted coupons overall
- Bar coupon acceptance was strongly tied to existing bar-going habits — people who visit bars more than once a month accepted at 0.769 vs 0.371 for less frequent visitors
- Age and passengers mattered too — drivers over 25 without kids were more likely to accept bar coupons
- Coffee house coupons followed a similar pattern — regular coffee drinkers accepted more, and morning coupons performed better
